Ex Astra

Ex Astra (Latin: "from the stars" (ex-from) (astra-stars (pl.)) was founded on May 3rd, 2021.

We are a fast-growing guild.

Our goal is to be a supportive community for our guildmembers. We aim to be helpful citizens of Norrath, while fostering a culture of patience, teamwork, and exceptional group accomplishments.

We are an explorative guild conducting regular raids. Teamwork, cooperation & diplomacy are extremely important to us, & we look to bring value to any group effort.

We are an adventurous group, and look to add a bit of a breath of fresh air in the world around us.
